Biography

About me

Hi, I'm Miguel, a mastering engineer based in Porto, Portugal. I've been mastering records since 2010. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with both major and independent labels, collaborating with emerging artists as well as established acts, and mastering a wide range of releases across various genres.

I'm one of the founding members of Estúdios Sá da Bandeira, Bender Mastering Studio and Arda Recorders, where I've had my mastering room since the beginning of my career. I'm also the founder of Knurl Mastering, where I currently master records.
