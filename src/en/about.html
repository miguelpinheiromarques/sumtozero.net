Biography

A little bit about me

Hi, I’m Miguel. For over two decades, I have been specializing in the pro audio industry as a mastering engineer, studio designer, and educator. Whether shaping sound, designing the spaces where it is created, or teaching the next generation of engineers, my work is defined by a passion for sonic excellence. Feel free to explore the page to get to know me and the specifics of my work.

Mastering

My work as a mastering engineer

I've been mastering records since 2010. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with both major and independent labels, collaborating with emerging artists as well as established acts, and mastering a wide range of releases across various genres.

I'm one of the founding members of Estúdios Sá da Bandeira, Bender Mastering Studio and Arda Recorders, where I've had my mastering room since the beginning of my career. I'm also the founder of Knurl Mastering, where I currently master records.
